Quantum Information & Quantum Computing

Quantum information studies how information is stored at the atomic and subatomic levels, and quantum computing studies how to use that information to solve practical problems. Quantum information is stored differently than classical information, and one can use that fact to solve problems that would be difficult to solve classically. These areas are of great interest; you can find the latest research here.
