Particle physics

Particle physics is the study of the fundamental constituents of matter. We describe these fundamental constituents in a model called the Standard Model. The Standard Model is similiar to the Periodic table - as the Periodic table categorizes elements in chemistry by their important properties, the Standard Model categorizes particles in physics by their important properties: mass, charge, spin, etc. You can find some of the latest research here.
