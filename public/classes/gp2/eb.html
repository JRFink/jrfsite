Electromagnetic Waves

In order to understand wave-particle duality The first technical description of light as a wave came in Christiaan Huygens paper Treatise on Light in 1678. He starts with experience, and says that there has not been a sufficient explanation as to why light particles can pass through one another without interfering with eachother. He makes other observations about light, and using geometry goes on to describe his wave theory of light.
